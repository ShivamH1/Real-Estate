About us

Rooted in Pune, built on referrals

Samarth Corporation began with a simple observation: families in Pune didn’t need more listings — they needed someone they could trust to read the fine print with them.

Over a decade later, most of our business still comes the old-fashioned way: a family we helped telling another family about us. We keep our portfolio residential-first and our promises in writing — because in this business, your name travels faster than any advertisement.

Our mission is straightforward: make every property transaction in Pune feel as safe as it should be.
