About

Fourteen years of Pune real estate

Saamarth Properties is the practice of one belief — that a broker’s real job is protecting the client’s decision, not pushing a sale.

Since 2012, I’ve helped families and investors buy, sell and lease property across East Pune. Most of my work still comes from referrals — families I’ve helped telling other families about me. That only happens when advice is honest, paperwork is clean, and the relationship outlasts the transaction.

Mission

To empower clients with transparent advice, market expertise and end-to-end real estate solutions — building lasting relationships by helping every customer make informed property decisions, with trust, integrity and professionalism.

Vision

To be the most trusted, customer-centric real estate brand — delivering transparent, innovative and value-driven property solutions while helping clients build wealth through real estate.
